How to create a merchant account

1. Click create a merchant

This will take you to a wizard to create a merchant.

2. Enter merchant infomation

You will need merchant information to complete this section.

3. Enter merchant contact infomation

You will need merchant contact information to complete this section.

4. Enter merchant financial infomation

You will need merchant financial information in order to process payments section. Click complete and the merchant will be created! You can find the merchant account under your section titled Merchant > List .
